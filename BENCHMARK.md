## Conclusions

1. **VIBEE adds negligible overhead** (~1ms, <0.1% of total pipeline)
2. **Native targets (C, Zig, Rust)** deliver best runtime performance
3. **Go and Java** offer good balance of performance and productivity
4. **Interpreted languages** are 50-200x slower for compute-intensive tasks
5. **VIBEE enables target flexibility** without specification changes
