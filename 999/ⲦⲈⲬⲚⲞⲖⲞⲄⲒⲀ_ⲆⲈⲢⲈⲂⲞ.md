### E-Graph & Equality Saturation

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|------------------|
| **egg: Fast and Extensible Equality Saturation** | Willsey, Nandi, Wang, Flatt, Tatlock, Panchekha | POPL 2021 | E-class analyses, rebuilding technique, 2.5x speedup |
| **Relational E-Matching** | Zhang, Wang, Willsey, Tatlock | POPL 2022 | Worst-case optimal join for pattern matching |
| **egglog: Datalog + Equality Saturation** | Zhang, Wang, Flatt, Cao, Zucker, Rosenthal, Tatlock, Willsey | PLDI 2023 | Unifies Datalog with EqSat, incremental execution |

**arXiv Links:**
- [arXiv:2004.03082](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.03082) - egg paper
- [arXiv:2108.02290](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.02290) - Relational E-Matching
- [arXiv:2304.04332](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.04332) - egglog

### Quantization & Ternary Neural Networks

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|------------------|

| **AWQ: Activation-aware Weight Quantization** | Lin, Tang, Tang, Yang, Chen, Wang, Xiao, Dang, Gan, Han | MLSys 2024 | 1% salient weights, 3x speedup |
