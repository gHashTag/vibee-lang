## HNSW Papers

### 1. HNSW: Hierarchical Navigable Small World
**Malkov & Yashunin 2018** (IEEE TPAMI)
- Paper: "Efficient and robust approximate nearest neighbor search using HNSW"
- Key: Graph-based ANN with O(log n) search
- Citation: 2000+

### 2. NSW: Navigable Small World
**Malkov et al. 2014**
- Paper: "Approximate nearest neighbor algorithm based on navigable small world graphs"
- Key: Foundation for HNSW

## Quantization Papers

### 3. INT8 Quantization
**Jacob et al. 2018** (CVPR)
- Paper: "Quantization and Training of Neural Networks for Efficient Integer-Arithmetic-Only Inference"
- Key: Symmetric/asymmetric INT8

### 4. Product Quantization
**Jégou et al. 2011** (IEEE TPAMI)
- Paper: "Product Quantization for Nearest Neighbor Search"
- Key: Subspace quantization, ADC

### 5. Optimized Product Quantization
**Ge et al. 2014** (CVPR)
- Paper: "Optimized Product Quantization"
- Key: Rotation optimization for PQ

### 6. Binary Hashing
**Gong et al. 2013** (CVPR)
- Paper: "Iterative Quantization: A Procrustean Approach to Learning Binary Codes"
- Key: ITQ for binary embeddings
