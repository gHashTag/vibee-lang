# PART III: EXCEPTIONAL JORDAN ALGEBRA

## 1. Definition

J₃(8) is the algebra of 3×3 Hermitian matrices over octonions.

### 1.1 Dimension

dim(J₃(8)) = 27 = 3³ (Trideyatoe!)

### 1.2 Structure

$$X = \begin{pmatrix} \alpha & a & \bar{b} \\ \bar{a} & \beta & c \\ b & \bar{c} & \gamma \end{pmatrix}$$

Where α, β, γ ∈ R and a, b, c ∈ O.

## 2. Characteristic Equation

$$\lambda^3 - \text{Tr}(X)\lambda^2 + S(X)\lambda - \det(X) = 0$$

The three eigenvalues correspond to three generations!
