## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines

**Good**:
- "VIBEE - Evolutionary Compiler (Seeking $750K)"
- "Quick Demo - 2.5x Faster Than Rust"
- "Following Up - VIBEE Seed Round"

**Bad**:
- "Investment Opportunity"
- "Check This Out"
- "Quick Question"

### Opening Lines

**Good**:
- "I'm Dmitrii Vasilev, founder of VIBEE..."
- "Thanks for the great conversation today..."
- "Following up on my email from last week..."

**Bad**:
- "I hope this email finds you well..."
- "I wanted to reach out to you about..."
- "I'm writing to you because..."

### Call to Action

**Good**:
- "Would you be interested in a 15-minute demo?"
- "Can we schedule a call for [date/time]?"
- "Let me know if you need anything else!"

**Bad**:
- "Let me know your thoughts."
- "Looking forward to hearing from you."
- "Please advise."

### Closing

**Good**:
- "Best, Dmitrii"
- "Thanks! Dmitrii"
- "Looking forward to it! Dmitrii"

**Bad**:
- "Sincerely,"
- "Regards,"
- "Cheers,"

## Response Time Guidelines

**Initial Outreach**: Wait 1 week before follow-up  
**After Intro Call**: Follow up within 24 hours  
**During Due Diligence**: Respond within 4 hours  
**Term Sheet Negotiation**: Respond within 24 hours  
**Monthly Updates**: Send on first Monday of month
