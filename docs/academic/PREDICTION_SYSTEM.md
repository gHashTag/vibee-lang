2. Identify the **Transformer** (the pattern/law)
3. Extrapolate to predict the **Result**

## 10. Limitations

### 10.1 What This System Cannot Do

- **Prove** mathematical theorems (only predict)
- **Guarantee** predictions are correct
- **Replace** experimental verification
- **Predict** truly novel phenomena (no pattern to extrapolate)

### 10.2 Sources of Error

- **Incomplete patterns**: Missing data leads to wrong extrapolation
- **Non-linear effects**: Linear extrapolation fails for complex systems
- **Phase transitions**: Abrupt changes break smooth patterns
- **Unknown unknowns**: Phenomena outside known patterns
